The Founder

Rishay —
Founder &
Director.

I'm Rishay, founder of Vanguard Studios and a KZN-based videographer with a background in real estate content that goes deeper than most.

I grew up watching this province get sold short. Not by the agents — most of them are exceptional at what they do — but by the content. Static photos taken in a hurry. Drone footage uploaded raw. Listings going live with nothing that made a buyer feel anything.

And in a world where the first showing happens on a phone screen at 11pm on a Tuesday, that matters more than most agents realise.

Vanguard Studios exists to close that gap. Every shoot I do is built around one question: what would make a buyer feel something? Not just see a house — but want to live there. That's the difference between a reel that performs and one that disappears.

If that sounds like what you've been looking for — let's talk.
